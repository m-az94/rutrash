r u trash? a trash checker

Hi Bookish Girlies! This is a quick website I put together so you can easily look up whether or not your favourite authors are Treasures or Trash, based on their stance on the genocide in Gaza. I'll work on it more and add features like brands, celebrities, add searchability, etc. after midterms.

TO BE CLEAR: This website does not condone any hateful speach or actions against the Jewish people.

I hope this helps with future book purchases. Please consider helping me out by informing me when more authors speak out on either stance here.
